The Weekly Toolbox (WN32)

This is The Weekly Toolbox, week 32 of 2021. Containers and mesh networks are theme this week.

naml

If we are kindred spirits then you may find YAML deplorable too. When doing work related to Kubernetes then it’s unavoidable. Luckily someone went enough! and created naml. Go programs instead of YAML, finally some sanity. The author has empathy for people with substantial piles of YAML. The program can “codify” existing YAML to Go.

LXD

You might be wondering why an old tool like LXD is on the list this week. I’ve known about it for years but haven’t looked into it deeper besides the obvious association with LXC and the cursory glance at their homepage. I was working on a container tool called iyclo and just realized that LXD is very close to what I’m envisioning for iyclo. I’m putting it on hiatus until I can figure out what significantly differentiates it or if it improves upon LXD in any way.

Netmaker

Mesh networks are hot right now. The project page of Netmaker indicates that it’s WireGuard based and written in Go. I’m not familiar with SSPL license so that might be a deal breaker for some deployments. I have to try it out and see how it compares to ZeroTier which I have extensively used in the past.
